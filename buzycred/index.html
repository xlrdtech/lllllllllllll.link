Common Questions

Does this require a Personal Guarantee (PG)?

Our goal is to help you build credit that requires NO Personal Guarantee. However, some advanced cash loans may require one depending on the lender.

How long does it take?

Typically, clients see their first vendor accounts reporting in 30-60 days. Access to cash credit usually happens around month 6-9 if you follow the steps.

Is this credit repair?

No. This is Business Credit Building. We are building a brand new credit profile for your EIN that is separate from your personal SSN.
