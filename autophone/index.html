Master Your Trade, Delegate the Tech

Whether you're a cosmetologist creating the perfect look, a contractor building a solid foundation, or a retail owner curating unique products, your passion and skill are what drive your business. What you're likely not an expert in is the complex world of business technology, with all its jargon about tech stacks, workflows, and integrations.

This guide is designed to translate that confusing tech talk into simple, practical terms. Its goal is to show you how a new, automated phone system can become your most reliable employee, handling routine tasks so you can get back to doing what you love.

What is "Business Automation"?

Think of It as Your 24/7 Virtual Assistant

At its core, business automation is about setting up systems to handle repetitive tasks for you. This assistant never sleeps, never takes a break, and handles customer interactions perfectly every time.

Intake

Welcomes new customers, handles initial consultations, gathers essential client info, and gets them started without you picking up the phone.

Order Fulfillment

Takes orders directly over the phone. A customer speaks their order, and the assistant processes it, updating your sheets automatically.

Outreach

Acts as marketing. Hosts phone-based webinars or shares important info. Customers just dial in to listen—no internet required.

Meet the Tech Team

A "tech stack" is just a team of specialized digital tools. You don't need to be an expert in how they work, just understand their basic roles.

Jitsi Meet

The Switchboard Operator

Provides the main phone number for customers to call into.

Loopback

The Connector

Routes the audio from the customer's call directly to the AI agent.

Comet Browser + Perplexity AI

The Smart Assistant

Listens, understands, and instantly takes action in your business systems.

macOS Server

The Office

Where the whole team works from, ensuring everything is up and running.

A Customer's Journey

Meet Sarah, a returning customer placing an order.

1

Sarah Dials In

Sarah calls your business number. The system instantly recognizes her number and pulls her profile.

2

Personalized Greeting

"Hi Sarah, welcome back! Are you looking to reorder your usual, or try something new?"

3

Natural Conversation

Sarah speaks naturally. The AI understands her request and confirms details based on her history.

4

Instant Fulfillment

The AI updates your spreadsheets/CRM instantly. Sarah hangs up happy, and you get a ready-to-go order.

The best part? This is entirely no-code for you. Effortless to run.
