Site Preparation
Guide for 30213

Cascade Palmetto Hwy features Georgia red clay. Do not place assets directly on ground. Follow this deployment sequence:

  • 01

    Leveling & Racking

    Site must be perfectly level. If the container is "twisted," doors will not close.

  • 02

    Elevation Blocks

    Use 6"x6" pressure-treated ties or concrete blocks at 4 corners to prevent floor rot.

  • 03

    Gravel Pad (CR-6)

    Prevents the 15-ton delivery truck from sinking into clay during wet cycles.
