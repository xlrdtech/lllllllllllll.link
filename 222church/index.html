Our Story

222 Church is a Spirit-filled community where followers of Jesus gather to worship the one true God and encounter His presence.

Identity

We exist to help people walk in their identity, purpose, and calling through relational discipleship, authentic community, biblical teaching, and Spirit-led worship.

Whether you are beginning your journey with Christ or seeking deeper spiritual formation, 222 Church is a place to worship, grow, be equipped, and live on mission.

We Are Rooted

  • Worship that honors the one true God
  • Biblical and apostolic teaching
  • Prayer and spiritual disciplines
  • Intentional discipleship and equipping

We Live on Mission

  • Carrying God’s presence into everyday life
  • Making disciples in homes and communities
  • Serving with humility and love
  • Passing the faith on to future generations
